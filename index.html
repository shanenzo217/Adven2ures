Content of our Channel

Adven2ures is a channel run by students from Grade 10 at APEC Schools Las Pinas.The main goal of Adven2ures is to show the students' daily activities and experiences, especially as they relate to student life. Our channel aims to provide viewers an insight into the everyday routines, challenges, and joys of being a student.

What does our Stream provide?

Just recently, they posted their video “A day in the life of an APEC student”, which shows different routines, different hobbies, and different levels of productivity. The premiere video showed what it is like to be an APEC student and what a “typical’ student does in their everyday life. The video showed 6 different students’ daily lives, and it can be seen that the students have similarities and differences.
